About Us

Company History

AMR Global Solutions was established through partnership between 3 well Experienced Professionals who are experts in I.T. Solutions, HR Shared services and Supply chain network.

We aim to help our clients innovate and create cost effective and high valuable approach to bring them to the competitive edge.

We provide tailor-made solutions to local and international Companies and provide assistance to foreign and start-up companies doing business in the Philippines.

Our Mission

Make it as simple and seamless as possible for our clients to set up, improve and do business in the Philippines by providing all needed services and advice allowing the clients to focus on their human capital, operations and revenue.
